Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients:
  - Peanut Butter
  - Jelly
  - Two slices of bread

Directions:
  1. Take a spoonful of Peanut Butter and spread it generously on a slice of bread.
  2.  Take one spoonful of Jelly and spread it on top of the the slice of bread on which you spread the peanut butter.
  3.  Take the second slice of bread and place it on top of the slice of bread which contains the peanut butter and jelly.
  4.  Enjoy your sandwich!
